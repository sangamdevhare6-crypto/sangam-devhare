Before you ask support

Common questions

Can I keep my number?+
Yes. Porting takes under 24 hours, and your old plan stays active until the switch completes.
Is there a contract?+
No. Every plan is month-to-month. Cancel or change tiers anytime from the app.
What happens if I use all my data?+
Speeds drop to 1 Mbps until your next cycle, or top up instantly in the app for no extra fee.
Does the eSIM work on my phone?+
Most phones from 2019 onward support eSIM. We check compatibility automatically during signup.
